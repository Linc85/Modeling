About Lincoln

Lincoln Brown is a professional model that has done a variety of print, suit, runway and fitness modeling as well as a little bit of acting. Lincoln graduated from Kansas State University in 2016 with a Bachelor of Science Degree in Business Administration with an emphasis in Management Information Systems.

In 2013 Lincoln began working part time at Hollister with the job title as “Model”, later that year he met Janis Kliethermes who signed him to a contract with The Agency Models & Talent in Kansas City. Janis has since departed and left the Agency to Becky Grover whom Lincoln directly works with.

Lincoln is currently an IT Project Management Analyst working in downtown Kansas City, with experience executing Data and Business Analyst responsibilities. He has always been interested in computers and has recently began been taking courses in Front-end software development languages such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript. The next steps are to begin learning backend languages like Java, C# & Python.
